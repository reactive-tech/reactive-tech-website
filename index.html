We develop resilient and customer oriented applications and support the growth of our customers' businesses. We offer long term support for the products and services that we provide.

We are also actively developing in house applications and iterating on new business ideas.

Our expertise

We specialise in data encryption, Kubernetes, PostgreSql, MySql, Go, Java, JS, Html5, Python, PHP, Flutter mobile development, Open Source contributions, Scrum and teams organisations.

In addition to developing apps, we specialise in setting-up tailored clouds for our customers using bare metal and open-source techs like Kubernetes. This approach allows our customers to have full control about where geographically their data is stored and prevent them from being locked in a proprietary cloud providers API.
